Invitation to attend  Recommerce – Global Battery Recycling Conference

Venue: The Lalit Ashok, Bengaluru & Online

Date: 3rd & 4th of March 2021  

The event is officially supported by Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Karnataka State Pollution Control Board, Bureau of Indian Standards and Digital India. The objective of the event is to encourage recycling of batteries, increase awareness about battery recycling, promote business co-operation and development in the areas of battery recycling and EV battery recycling in India.

This platform is putting together 25+ eminent speakers , and panelist(Policy Makers, Industry experts )across the Globe. The conference will give perspective on Battery Recycling in India and Globe, insights about the opportunities and challenges for the entire value chain.

Delegates will have opportunity to attend physical and virtual and engage, discuss and network with the stall worlds of the Industry.
